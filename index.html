Our Roadmap

Phase 1: Launch and Initial Development

(February 2023 - May 2023)

Launch the CorePanda token on the Core blockchain.

Airdrop tokens to early community members and supporters to incentivize engagement and adoption.

Establish partnerships with other blockchain projects and crypto communities to increase awareness and adoption.

Phase 2: Integration and Expansion

(June 2023 - September 2023)

Integrate CorePanda into various decentralized finance (DeFi) applications to increase utility and demand for the token.

Launch staking and farming programs to incentivize long-term holding and community participation.

Continue to establish partnerships and collaborations with other blockchain projects and communities.

Phase 3: Governance and Community Empowerment

(October 2023 - January 2024)

Launch a governance model for CorePanda that allows token holders to vote on key decisions and proposals.

Develop a community grant program to fund and support innovative projects and initiatives related to CorePanda.

Launch a decentralized autonomous organization (PandaDAO) to further empower the community and allow for decentralized decision-making.

Host events and meetups to build relationships with the community and increase engagement.

CorePanda is a next-generation cryptocurrency built on the Core blockchain. Our mission is to make cryptocurrency more accessible and fun by creating a token that is not only valuable, but also cute and cuddly. We believe that by offering a wide range of use cases and incentives, we can help to drive adoption of the Core blockchain and promote the growth of the cryptocurrency market as a whole.
CorePanda is built on the Core blockchain, which is known for its security, speed, and scalability.
The token is an ERC-20 token, which means that it is compatible with a wide range of wallets and exchanges.
CorePanda holders can earn rewards by staking their tokens, but the reward rate will change depending on the overall staking rate of the network. This helps to incentivize more people to stake their tokens and secure the network.
The total supply of CorePanda tokens is 10 billion.
50% burning of Token and ongoing sales through decentralized exchanges (DEXs). The token burning will happen in phases as we reach milestone of the project development, with the proceeds being used to fund development and marketing of the project.
The token can be used for a variety of purposes, including:Transactions, Staking, Governance, Liquidity provision, NFTs.
CorePanda tokens can be used as a form of payment for goods and services within the Core ecosystem.
